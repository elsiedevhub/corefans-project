Our story

Started From The Bottom

Corefans began as a small idea in 2011, an answer to the question - How can creators make the money they need to keep creating? We had watched young artists struggle to break into success. We had seen creators take on side-jobs to get the money they needed to fuel their passion, We had observed artists having to sell out so they can make a living, And we wanted to end that. We wanted creatives to get returns on thr tremendous effort, passion and sweat they put into their art. So we got to work and in 2021, Corefans emerged.

About Corefans

Corefans is a one-stop suite for emerging, creative talents to earn, make sense of their finances, and become intentional about fanbase-building. We use an invite-only platform that lets account managers recruit emerging cretive talents and support them in diversifying their revenue streams through cash tips, mersch, store sales and ticket sales. In this way, creators get paid to keep creatiing by the people they create for. For emerging creative talents, the support of Corefans is extremely important. This is why we are obsessed with developing tools and resources to help them earn more and succeed, not just through streaming but through alternative fan-powered streams. We help creatives fund their hustle, One corefan at a time.

Why Corefans

Talent Obsessed

Not to sound like stalkers, but we are obsessed with finding, nurturing and rewarding talent all over the world. We are obsessed with helping them receive more value so they can provide more value. We are obsessed with their growth and finances, and we spend hours every day thinking about better solutions for creators who put in the work tirelessly to create the art, content, and music that we love.

Laser-Focused

We’ve got our eye on the prize and we’re not looking away. We know what we want and we work hard to make sure we get it. For us, the prize is creators getting the flowers they deserve.
